  1. Hall ticket should be preserved till the declaration of results.

  3. University reserves the right to cancel the admission of the candidates at any stage, when it is detected that his/her admission to the examination or the college is against the rules.
  4. Candidates should occupy their seats at least 15 minutes before the commencement of the examination.
  5. Candidates are prohibited from bringing and using printed/written material of any kind into the Exam Hall.
  6. Candidates are prohibited from bringing Mathematical Tables, however, if required will be supplied by the Controller of Examiner. Students of Mathematics, Science and Engineering are allowed to bring their own non programmable calculator.
  7. No Electronic gadgets/Mobiles are permitted in the exam Hall. If found in the exam hall booked under malpractice.
  8. Candidates if found in copying/communicating with others or indulging in any malpractice will be expelled from the examination Hall and will not be allowed to appear for the remaining papers/subjects.
  9. Candidates are required to bring their Hall Ticket and ID cards every day.
  10. Candidate should not write anything except his / her hall ticket number on Question Paper.
  11. Student will not be allowed to leave the examination hall during the first two hours of the examination.
